About

Team Quantaverse is a dynamic group of students under the Uunchai initiative, working at the forefront of quantum computing to explore and solve complex real-world challenges. With a strong focus on the Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP) and its optimization through quantum simulation techniques, the team leverages tools like Qiskit to experiment with quantum algorithms that promise faster and more efficient solutions. The project is guided by the dedicated mentorship of Yubraj Bhandari and Diya Pandey, whose expertise and vision continually inspire the team to push boundaries, think innovatively, and contribute meaningfully to the future of technology.

Project

The project focuses on the Traveling Salesman Problem (TSP), a classic optimization problem in computer science and operations research. The goal is to find the shortest possible route that visits a set of cities and returns to the origin city. The team will explore quantum simulation techniques using Qiskit, a powerful open-source quantum computing framework, to develop algorithms that can efficiently solve TSP instances. By leveraging the principles of quantum mechanics, the team aims to enhance the performance of classical algorithms and contribute to advancements in quantum computing.
