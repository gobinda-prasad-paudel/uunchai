FAQs

Eligibility
  • Are there any requirements to participate in the program?
    expand_more expand_less
    Anyone who is studying grade 8,9,10,11, 12 or in their gap years is eligible to participate in the program. The applicant must not have enrolled in a Bachelor’s program. Students must have access to a computer and a stable internet connection for video calls. Please contact us if you want to participate in this program but cannot meet this requirement.
  • What are the time commitments like?
    expand_more expand_less
    Students are required to commit at least 13 hours per week to the program. The more time you can put into the program, the more you'll likely get out of the program.
    • Program Overview
  • Is the program free?
    expand_more expand_less
    Yes! There is absolutely no cost to attend the program.
  • Where will the program be held?
    expand_more expand_less
    This program will be conducted virtually. All communication will happen via video calls.
  • How will the 6 weeks of the program be organized?
    expand_more expand_less
    In the first week, students and mentors meet within their individual teams to discuss their approach to completing the project. The teams will begin their projects on day one. During this week, there will also be introductions, games, and learning sessions for the entire cohort. From the 2nd to the 5th week, teams will focus on their projects under the guidance of their assigned mentors. The 6th week will be dedicated to wrapping up the projects. At the end of the program, there will be a project showcase featuring distinguished guests. Team members will have flexible working hours but must attend weekly meetings. Upon completion of the program, mentors will recommend their cohorts to our internship partner companies.
    • Projects
  • Do we get to choose projects of our interests or are they assigned randomly?
    expand_more expand_less
    You will be assigned to projects based on your interests and skills that are shown on your application.
